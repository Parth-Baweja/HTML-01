Description

ILFORD HP5 PLUS 400 Film is one of the most popular black-and-white films available, loved by photographers for its flexibility and consistent results. With an ISO of 400, it performs well in various lighting conditions, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor photography. The fine grain and wide exposure latitude allow for great detail and sharpness, whether you’re shooting portraits, landscapes, or street photography. This film is perfect for those who want to explore the timeless art of black-and-white photography.
