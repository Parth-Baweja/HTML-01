About Steve Lacy

Steve Lacy is a musician, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He gained prominence as the guitarist of the Grammy-nominated band The Internet, known for their fusion of R&B, soul, and neo-soul. Lacy is also recognized for his solo work, characterized by his unique blend of funk, jazz, and alternative R&B. He released his debut solo project, "Steve Lacy's Demo," in 2017, which received critical acclaim. Lacy is known for his innovative use of guitar effects and his distinctive falsetto vocals. He has collaborated with various artists across different genres and continues to explore and push boundaries in his music.
