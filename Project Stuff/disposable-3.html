Description

The Analog Disposable Camera is designed for those who want to dive into film photography without a significant investment. This camera is preloaded with 35mm film and features a fixed focus lens, making it incredibly easy to use. The lightweight design and built-in flash make it ideal for capturing memories at parties, on vacations, or during any special occasion. The photos have a charming, nostalgic quality, bringing the magic of film to your everyday life.

Technical Specifications

Film Type 35mm
Lens Fixed focus

Flash Built-in flash
Battery Included, single use
